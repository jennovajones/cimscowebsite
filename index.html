Cimsco are a Wellington based company with a national presence. We've been working in the electricity industry for years, and we have a great understanding of the electricity needs of large and small organisations.

We take the information contained in your electricity bill, and turn it into easy to understand graphics. This will help you make better decisions about the electricity you use, and can even help you chose a new provider when the time comes.
